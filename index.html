Where We Could Have Met

A little page for you — a mix of English & Arabic, just how I feel.

Sometimes I wonder what you truly are to me.
Every time the answer feels bigger than words.
You are the quiet miracle my heart keeps choosing.
Even on the days when everything feels uncertain.
No matter the differences between us.
No matter the weight we carry.
Whether life shifts or stays painfully the same.
You remain the one thing I could never reach.
Yet the one I loved more deeply than you could ever imagine.
These thoughts do not visit me with anyone else.
They come only with you.
Suddenly and softly.
Like the way dusk arrives.
Without asking permission.
Yet impossible to ignore.
I imagine us in a quiet garden.
Roses moving gently around us.
Warm lights glowing like small constellations.
A blanket between us.
A playlist of songs that feel like whispered memories.
I bring the tea.
You bring your smile.
The smile that holds an entire universe inside it.
Would you meet me somewhere quiet.
Where even the flowers lean in to listen.
Because every time I think of you.
I discover a new reason to smile.
If love were a season.
I would choose to live in your summer forever.
Warm.
Bright.
Endless.
There are moments when I wish I could say all of this out loud.
Moments when my hands ache to write you letters on paper.
Real letters.
The kind people used to pour their whole hearts into.
I have written so many.
Folded them carefully.
Traced your name on the envelope.
But I never found the courage to send them.
Maybe because the words felt too honest.
Maybe because loving you feels too big to fit on a single page.
But here I am now.
Writing anyway.
Because the truth keeps rising in me.
No matter how much I try to quiet it.
The truth is this.
After everything.
After all the beginnings and endings.
After all the things we never said.
I still love you.
More softly.
More quietly.
More honestly than before.
Maybe this is the closest thing to courage I have ever had with you.
